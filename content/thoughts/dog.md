When seeking a role model, One might want to look at Dog.

Dog loves unconditionally. You are master? Dog loves you. You are stranger? Dog
loves you.

Dog looks past race and creed. Dog does not care if you are happy, sad, mean,
or mad.

Dog loves you for who you are, not what you do, or say. Dog is just simply,
Dog.

Human, take notes.
