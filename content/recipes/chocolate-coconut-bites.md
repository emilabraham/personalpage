---
title: "Chocolate Coconut Bites"
type: recipes
---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded sweetened coconut
- 2 tablespoon coconut oil
- 2 tablespoon honey
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar
- 1-2 drops vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup dark or semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Process the shredded coconut and brown sugar until it turns into a powder.
2. Mix the powder, honey, oil, and vanilla in a bowl.
3. Roll into spheres and place on wax/parchment paper in fridge for 10 minutes.
4. Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave, stirring every 30 seconds.
5. Dunk the spheres in the chocolate and place them back in the fridge for another 10 minutes.
